Interview Series

The Electrical Engineering Department of IIT Delhi is an enigma in its own right. The sheer variety of opportunities available sometimes makes it difficult even for the very best of students to decide their career path.
The Electrical Engineering Society, IIT Delhi presents an interview series with some of our most recent alumni who are making it big in the field of their choice. With diverse fields ranging from consulting, finance and of course Core Electrical, these interviews will answer a lot of questions that you might have had in your mind.
