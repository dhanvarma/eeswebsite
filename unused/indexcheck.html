About Us

Technical

The EES has something for every student. We conduct many lectures and hugely popular workshops on a myriad range of topics such as Machine Learning, Digital Electronics, Microcontrollers, Image Processing and even Ethical Hacking. The EES also holds technical competitions which provides students an opportunity to learn while also getting to win many exciting prizes. We also provide students the opportunity to work with our club through various projects and also participate in many technical fests held throughout the country.

Cultural

The EES holds many cultural events throughout the year. We start the academic year with the hugely popular EE Fresher's Party, which provides the fresherie of our department an opportunity to have fun while getting to know their peers. We also help students engage with our very talented seniors by publishing regular interviews and holding interactive sessions, where they get an opportunity to get their doubts cleared. Fun events such as LAN Gaming and Inter Departmental Sporting Events are also a regular part of our event calendar.

About Us

Electrical Engineering Society (EES), IIT Delhi seeks to promote interaction amongst students and faculty by organizing interactive events.
