CS Lan Gaming

EES presents LAN Gaming competition to cure your minor hangover.
Bring your laptops and participate in teams of 5 on October 12.
Enter the Karakoram CR at 9:30 pm to win exciting prizes worth Rs 9000.
It turned out to be an excellent Post Minors Entertainment.
Here are some photos of our LAN Gaming event. The participants got to take out their frustrations in life by blowing up stuff.
The winners were Shovan Paul, Utkarsh Sinha, Soham Desai, Sunny Bhagia and Vishwajeet Singh.

Gaming is an integral part of hostel life at IITD. In order to give the gaming culture a competitive touch, the EES held its first LAN Gaming event on 12th October 2015. The event proved to be hugely popular, witnessing participation of over 14 teams. The 5 team members format in Counterstrike proved to be a game changer.
After 3 highly competitive rounds, the event was finally won by a group of second year students. Encouraged by this huge response, we held another LAN Gaming event, this time in collaboration with ACES-ACM on 13th January 2016. Both events had cash prizes of over Rs 5000 each. The second year students once again proved their supremacy by winning this event as well. The winners were Shovan Paul, Utkarsh Sinha, Soham Desai, Sunny Bhagia and Vishwajeet Singh.
LAN Gaming has emerged to be one of the most popular cultural events of our club and we hope to conduct more such events in the future.
