Microcontroller Workshop

Have you always wondered how your calculator, game boy, digital watch or almost any other electronic device works?
Have you ever wanted to make your own game?
For you Electrical Engineering Society presents Workshop on Microcontrollers. Here we’ll start from the very basics, and build from there so that you can make own cool projects. As a part of the workshop, you’ll also be making your own game using microcontrollers!!
