Freshers' Night 2015

The night was a memorable one for the EE Freshers 2015. Freshers showed us that there is no dearth of talent in them and the audience enjoyed every performance. There was very tough competition for Mr and Miss Fresher.Finally:
Mr Fresher: Aishwary Srivastava
Miss Fresher: Sakshi Jain
