National Level Coding Contest 2016

Electrical Engineering Society, IIT Delhi in association with VBM Technologies, Noida is proud to announce NLCC-2016 (National Level Coding Contest). All the preliminary rounds will be held at various esteemed Engineering Institutes across INDIA in present academic year and the Grand Finale will be organized at IIT Delhi.

Highlights of Final Round at IIT-Delhi:

  • Winners of all Zonal Centers will be competing against each other at NLCC-2016 in March/April 2016 at IIT Delhi.
  • Winners of the Final Round will win The NLCC title and will be awarded and honored by EES IIT Delhi.
  • A certificate of Excellence will also be awarded to the winners from EES IIT Delhi.
  • Free STP (Summer Training Program) for the runner ups at VBM Technologies, Campus.
  • Chance to work as Intern with VBM Technologies Pvt. Ltd. on Real Projects of various MNCâ€™s.
  • Prizes worth Rupees 1,00,000 to be won including Laptops and Tablets, to be given to the winner as well runner ups.

Finale: March/April 2016
Venue: IIT Delhi
