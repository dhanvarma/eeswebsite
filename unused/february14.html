17 Feb       Fuel your Grey Matter at Tryst'14

“The mind is not a vessel to be filled, but a fire to be kindled.”

- Lucius Mestrius Plutarchus

The need of the hour is to ignite in each and every person, the desire to achieve their personal best. The desire to imagine and engineer change. Department of Electrical Engineering proudly brings to fuel your grey matter with all new and exciting challenges at Tryst’14!!

1. Metal Mine
2. Wire Sprint
3. De_Bug
4. Electro Quiz
